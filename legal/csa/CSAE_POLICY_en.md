# Child Safety Standards Policy — Rezonans

**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Effective Date:** March 15, 2026

---

## 1. Our Commitment

Rezonans ("we", "the app") has a zero-tolerance policy toward **Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)**. The safety and well-being of minors are of utmost importance to us. This document sets out the standards we enforce to protect children on our platform.

---

## 2. Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited on Rezonans:

- Any content depicting, promoting, or glorifying the sexual abuse or exploitation of minors (persons under 18 years of age).
- Grooming behaviors, including soliciting personal information from minors or attempting to establish inappropriate relationships with minors.
- Sharing, storing, distributing, or transmitting **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)** in any form.
- Using the platform to contact, recruit, exploit, or harm minors.
- Any content that sexualizes minors, directly or indirectly.

Violation of these rules will result in **immediate and permanent account termination** and will be reported to the appropriate authorities.

---

## 3. Age Verification and Restrictions

- Rezonans requires date of birth at registration and enforces an **18+ age requirement**.
- Users below 18 years of age are not permitted to create an account or use the service.
- Reported underage accounts are removed immediately upon verification.

---

## 4. Content Moderation

- User-generated content (profile photos, audio clips, bios) is subject to automated and manual review.
- We use industry-standard tools to detect and remove CSAM, including hash-matching technologies consistent with the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline standards.
- Reported content is reviewed by our trust and safety team within 24 hours.

---

## 5. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report suspected CSAE-related content or behavior through:

- **In-app report button** available on every user profile and message.
- **Email:** furkanpala404@gmail.com with subject line `[CSAE REPORT]`

We encourage all users to report any content or behavior that appears to exploit or harm minors.

---

## 6. Cooperation with Authorities

- We comply fully with applicable local and international laws regarding CSAE, including the laws of the Republic of Turkey and applicable EU/international frameworks.
- We will cooperate with law enforcement agencies and report confirmed CSAM to the **NCMEC CyberTipline** (for US-connected content) and relevant national authorities.
- We retain legally required records as mandated by applicable laws to assist investigations.

---

## 7. Employee and Developer Standards

- All team members and contractors who handle user data or have access to user content are required to comply with this policy.
- Any team member found to have violated these standards will be immediately removed and reported to the appropriate authorities.

---

- [NCMEC CyberTipline](https://www.missingkids.org/gethelpnow/cybertipline) — National Center for Missing & Exploited Children
