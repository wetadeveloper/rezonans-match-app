## Summary

Rezonans has a **zero-tolerance policy** toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE):

- **18+ only platform** — Age verification enforced at registration
- **Prohibited:** All CSAM, grooming, and exploitation of minors
- **Violations** result in immediate permanent ban and law enforcement reporting
- **Reporting:** In-app report button or email furkanpala404@gmail.com with subject `[CSAE REPORT]`
- **Standards aligned with:** NCMEC, IWF, Thorn, Google Play, and Apple App Store child safety policies

See the full policy documents linked above for complete details.
