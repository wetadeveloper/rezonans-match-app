- **İhlal/ban kaydı:** Tekrar kayıt yoluyla suistimali önlemek amacıyla imzalanmış (hash'lenmiş) form saklanabilir

## 🇬🇧 English

### How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Rezonans account and all associated data in two ways:

#### Method 1 — In-App (Instant)
1. Open the Rezonans app
2. Go to the **Profile** tab
3. Tap the **Settings** icon
4. Select **Delete Account**
5. Complete the confirmation steps

#### Method 2 — Request via Email
If you don't have access to the app or prefer email:

📧 **furkanpala404@gmail.com**

Write **"Account Deletion Request"** in the subject line and include:
- The **email address** registered to your account
- Your **username** (if applicable)

> **Response time:** Your request will be processed within **30 days**.

---

### Data That Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is **permanently removed**:

| Data Type | Deleted? |
|---|:---:|
| Profile information (name, email, date of birth, gender) | ✅ Yes |
| Profile photos | ✅ Yes |
| Voice profile recording | ✅ Yes |
| Message history | ✅ Yes |
| Likes and match history | ✅ Yes |
| Location data | ✅ Yes |
| Push notification token | ✅ Yes |
| Preferences and settings | ✅ Yes |
| Subscription / purchase history (RevenueCat) | ✅ Yes |

### Data That May Be Retained (Legal Requirement)

Only a minimum amount of data may be retained for legal obligations or legitimate interest:

- **Purchase records:** Retained for 5 years for tax and accounting purposes (does not include name/email, only transaction ID)
- **Abuse/ban record:** A hashed identifier may be retained to prevent re-registration abuse

---

### Deletion Timeline

```
Request received
     │
     ▼
Identity verification (for email requests)
     │
     ▼
Data removed from active systems — within 7 days
     │
     ▼
Cleaned from backup systems — within 30 days
     │
     ▼
All data permanently deleted ✅
```
