Optionally provided when creating your profile:

- Likes, super likes, pass actions

- Messaging history (stored encrypted)
- In-app purchase history (subscription status)

### 3.5 Device and Technical Data
- Device model and operating system version

- Crash reports and error logs (anonymous)
